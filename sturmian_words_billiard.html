About Sturmian Words

A Sturmian word is an infinite binary sequence with exactly n+1 factors of length n. In this billiard simulation, the tangent (tan θ) determines the slope of the ball's trajectory, and the sequence of wall hits creates a characteristic pattern. Try irrational values like √2 ≈ 1.414 or φ ≈ 1.618 (golden ratio) for non-repeating patterns.
